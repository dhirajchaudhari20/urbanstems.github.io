    Send Birthday Flowers Today

    Wish them a happy birthday in the brightest and most colorful way possible: with a beautiful flower arrangement! UrbanStems carries a wide assortment of celebratory bouquets that are perfect for the special occasion. Bring a big smile to a loved one’s face, be it a parent, a friend, a family member, or a co-worker. Everyone on your list will be delighted by the gift of fresh flowers, especially when you schedule same-day or next-day delivery so that it arrives right on time for the big day.

    Brighten Up Their Birthday

    Roses, lilies, peonies, and daisies are just a few of the vibrant stems you’ll find in this glorious array of birthday flowers. Befitting of the most important day of the year, these blooms brim with rich color, beautiful textures, and abundance—and they’ll definitely make a statement on their special day! Give a big birthday bouquet to honor a milestone birthday. Treat your parent to a thoughtfully arranged composition to celebrate them in style. Surprise a dear friend or a co-worker with a meaningful floral arrangement that warms their heart.

    Birthday Flowers for the Big Day

    When faced with so many fabulous choices, from mixed bouquets to uplifting sunflowers to eye-catching wildflowers, you might have trouble narrowing it down to just one. Make it simpler by purchasing flowers that reflect how you feel about the recipient. Red flowers evoke deep love and passion, while pink symbolizes friendship. Yellow is a happy hue that will make anyone smile big. Purple is perfect for the queen who reigns in your life. You can also select flowers by month, like the January carnation, the February iris, the June rose, and the October marigold. Add a balloon and some sweet treats and you’ve got yourself the perfect birthday gift for the one who means the most to you. At UrbanStems, you’ll find lush, fresh bouquets that capture the essence of your loved one and beautifully reflect how you feel. What could be better for their birthday?

    Birthday Flower FAQ's

    What types of flowers are best for birthdays?
    Anything goes when it comes to sending memorable floral arrangements for birthdays. Roses are always thoughtful, conveying your love and adoration in the most beautiful way possible. Lilies are powerfully symbolic of positivity and joy, which makes them equally perfect for brightening up their special day. For an instant uplift, send them a gorgeous birthday orchid. They require little maintenance, so they’re ideal for the celebrant whose green thumb needs a good workout. You can never go wrong with sunflowers, either. These brilliant blossoms instantly infuse their day with a touch of sunshine and happiness.

    What color flowers are best for birthdays?
    Color can convey so much. Bright and uplifting colors like yellow and pink are always welcome on a birthday. They’re naturally cheerful and sure to light up any space in an instant. If you prefer, choose the color based on their birth month. Rich tones dominate the fall and winter months, so you might opt for a deep red or a bold purple during those times of the year. Easing into spring, consider lighter and more radiant hues that resonate with the warmer weather and sunnier skies. Keep the breezy vibe going during summer, and opt for crisp hues that are the antithesis of the weather. Lots of lush green leaves lend a cool vibe to any bouquet, while colors like orange and fuchsia bring playful charm and optimism to their birthday flowers.

    What are birthday flowers by month?
    Each month has a flower with a specific quality. January’s flower is the humble carnation, symbolic of passion. In February, celebrate with the innocence of the primrose. March babies will smile big when you present them with a lovely violet. April celebrates with the lovely daisy, a true representative of springtime beauty. In May, it’s time to bring out lily of the valley for a stylish bouquet. June is the month of the honeysuckle, a wonderfully fragrant choice sure to please flower lovers. In July, leave some room for the lush water lily. August revolves around the radiant poppy, symbolic of true happiness. During September, the graceful morning glory takes over the spotlight. October’s child is ruled by calendula, an earthy choice suitable for someone always trying to find the right balance. The November birthday celebrant will be delighted to receive some fresh mums. By December, the holly arrives just in time to spread some signature goodwill and festive cheer.

    What are the best birthday flowers for friends?
    Some flowers are just perfect for sending your best birthday wishes to friends. Among them are sunflowers, which are symbolic of loyalty as well as good cheer. Sunflowers naturally turn in the direction of the sun, indicating a strong connection similar to the one you share with your friend. Mums are wonderful for friends, too, since they’re resonant with optimism. Yellow roses represent the strongest of friendships. No matter what you choose to send, you’re sure to find a lovely choice for anyone celebrating their special day.
